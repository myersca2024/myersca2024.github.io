About Me

Hello! My name is Caleb Myers, and I'm a 2nd year honors student studying Computer Science and Game Development at Northeastern University. Ever since I was little I've loved playing games, from classic tile-matching games like Tetris to popular hack-and-slash RPGs like Kingdom Hearts and everything inbetween. It's always been my goal to give back to the world what the games industry has given to me, and that is multiple unforgettable experiences in fun and immersion.
While I have basic experience in other aspects of game development like art and music, my main discipline is programming. Since starting my first project, I've participated in a few Game Jams and plan on entering many more both for fun and for practice. While most of my projects have been done in Unity, I'm interested and actively learning more avenues of game development, such as using C and gbdevkit to make Game Boy games.
When I'm not making games, I love playing them, hanging out with friends, and practicing my barista skills by making every coffee drink recipe I find!
