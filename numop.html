Numeral Operation

A puzzle game where adding and subtracting is the name of the game!

Description

Numeral Operation is a puzzle game in which the player is able to absorb and then disperse characters from themselves to add and subtract from the player's number. The player can utilize this to activate pressure plates, push heavy objects, and access elements not normally accessible to the player.

Details

Engine: Unity (2D)
Programming Language: C#
Role: Programmer, Designer, Artist

The Process

Numeral Operation was developed over the course of 36 hours during the final stretch of the 2021 Brackeys Game Jam. Due to school, I started the jam in the final days of its runtime. After starting, I came up with a basic puzzle concept that fit the jam's theme, "Stronger Together," and rapid prototyped features in a graybox scene. After implementing the mechanics, I developed the pixel art assets in paint.net while a friend offered to work on a simple music loop for the game. Numeral Operation was submitted with 2 hours left to spare.
