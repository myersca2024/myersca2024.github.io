Catherine Quest

A minigame collection featuring Catherine the Great as she tries to form a coup against her husband.

Description

Catherine Quest puts the player in 1762 in Moscow, Russia. They take the role of Catherine the Great, the eventual Tsarina of Russia, and her husband, Peter III, has just been coronated as Tsar of Russia. The player, as Catherine, must perform various tasks in order to build up a coup and overthrow Peter III and take control of Russia!

Details

Engine: Unity (3D)
Programming Language: C#
Role: Programmer, Designer
Available to play at itch.io

Contributions

  • Designed and implemented speech minigame
  • Implemented item and inventory system
  • Integrated UI assets

The Process

Catherine Quest's production started July 2020 and ended mid-August 2020 for a Game Programming class final project. Early in the semester, my peers and I brainstormed and submitted a design document that we would slowly implement into a game over the course of the semester as we learned new things in class. Using that knowledge I obtained in class, I implemented the final minigame of the game, a speech minigame, and the inventory and item system. After production finished, it was submitted to itch.io.
